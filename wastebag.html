WASTE BAG

Waste Bags

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality waste and recycling bags tailored for households, waste collection services, and waste management companies. These durable bags are designed to handle various types of waste and residues from residential, commercial, and construction activities.

Our waste collection bags provide an efficient solution for sorting and disposing of different waste materials, including cardboard, paper, glass, plastic, garden waste, and confidential documents.

To enhance usability, all our recycling bags can be custom-printed or embossed with logos, instructions, or content guidelines, ensuring proper waste segregation. The printed front panel serves as a clear indicator of what materials CAN and CANNOT be disposed of in the bag, helping to promote responsible waste management practices.

Applications
  • Also known as Kerbside Bags, commonly used for waste collection and recycling.
  • Widely used by households and municipal corporations for collecting garden waste.
  • Ideal for disposing of grass, flowers, lawn clippings, weeds, pruned branches, shrubs, and twigs.
  • Enhanced Features – Designed with a weighted rubber base for self-standing support and easy filling.
  • Versatile Construction Options – Available in U-Panel, 4-Panel, Circular, and Tubular designs with a single joint.
  • Customizable Colors – Available in various shades, including White, Green, Blue, and Orange.
  • Secure Closure System – Equipped with a Velcro top flap for easy closing and protection.
  • High-Quality Printing – Supports up to 4-side, 4-color printing for branding or waste segregation instructions.
  • Convenient Handling & Emptying – Standard design includes 2 lifting loops and a base strap for effortless unloading.
