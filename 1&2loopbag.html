1&2 Loop Bags

Single Loop Bags feature a single lifting loop integrated into the main fabric, while Double Loop Bags come with two lifting loops for enhanced handling flexibility. Unlike traditional four-loop bags, these designs offer a cost-effective alternative while ensuring efficient storage and transportation of granular and powdered bulk materials.

Also known as Fertilizer Bags, these 1 & 2 loop bulk bags are available in capacities ranging from 500 kg to 2000 kg and can be customized to meet specific industry needs. They consist of a durable outer bag with built-in lifting loops, which eliminates the need for separate handles or lifting accessories.

Depending on customer requirements, these bulk bags can be manufactured with coated or uncoated fabric and may include an optional inner liner for additional protection. Filling and discharge spouts are also available for controlled material flow.

Ideal for industries such as agriculture, construction, and manufacturing, these bags are commonly used for fertilizer, seeds, animal feed, cement, minerals, and more. Their innovative design ensures easy handling, secure lifting, and optimal load distribution, making them a preferred choice for bulk packaging solutions.

Characteristics

  • Provides a cost-effective and efficient bulk-handling solution for transporting, storing, and protecting various materials.
  • Made from 100% Virgin Polypropylene (PP), ensuring durability and strength, with a Safe Working Load (SWL) range of 500 kg to 1500 kg.
  • Designed to optimize truck and container loading, ensuring maximum space utilization and cost savings.
  • Suitable for a wide range of bulk materials, including fertilizers, animal feed, seeds, cement, minerals, chemicals, and food products.
  • Available with or without an additional coating for enhanced protection.
