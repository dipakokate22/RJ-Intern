GARDEN BAGS

Garden Bag

Garden Bags are specifically designed for nurseries and home gardening, providing an efficient solution for growing plants like tomatoes, ornamental plants, and salad crops. Made from non-toxic, lightweight materials, these bags ensure easy portability while promoting healthy plant growth.

Engineered for durability and leak resistance, Garden Bags offer optimal aeration and moisture retention, creating the perfect environment for rapid plant development. Their sturdy construction makes them a long-lasting and reliable choice for both commercial and personal gardening needs.

Applications
  • Also known as Kerbside Bags, commonly used for gardening and waste collection.
  • Ideal for disposing of grass, flowers, lawn clippings, weeds, tree prunings, shrubs, twigs, and small branches.
  • Widely used by households, municipal corporations, and gardening professionals for efficient waste management.
  • Available in customized colors, including White, Green, Blue, and Orange.
  • Versatile Construction – Offered in U-Panel, 4-Panel, Circular, and Tubular designs with a single joint.
  • High-Quality Printing – Supports up to 4-side, 4-color printing for branding or labeling.
  • Convenient Handling – Comes with 2 lifting loops and a base strap for easy emptying.
  • Secure Closure System – Equipped with a Velcro top flap to keep contents intact.
  • Enhanced Features – Designed with a weighted rubber base, allowing it to stand upright and stay open for easy filling.
