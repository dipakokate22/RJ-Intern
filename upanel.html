UPANEL BAGS

U Panel Bags

We provide a premium selection of U-Panel Corner Loop Bags, designed to ensure maximum safety and durability for high working load capacities. These U-Panel Bulk Bags (FIBC) feature a unique "U" shape, created using a single piece of fabric that forms the bottom and two opposite side panels.

With two additional side panels sewn to the structure, these bags attain a stable square shape when filled. The form stability can be further enhanced by adding inner baffles. Cost-effective and highly durable, U-Panel bags are an excellent choice for secure and efficient bulk packaging solutions.

Characteristics
  • Made from 100% virgin polymer, crafted into durable flat-woven polypropylene material.
  • U-Panel design consists of a single continuous fabric in a U-shape, with two side panels stitched securely for structural integrity.
  • High load-bearing capacity with reinforced corner loops for enhanced lifting strength.
  • Customizable width ranging from 90cm to 100cm, tailored to specific requirements.
  • Safe Working Load (SWL) options from 500kg to 2500kg, ensuring reliable performance.
  • Compatible with multiple filling and discharge configurations for versatile applications.
  • Safety Factor (SF): Available in 5:1 or 6:1 for superior safety standards.
  • Recognized as a globally accepted industry-standard packaging solution
Benefits
  • Exceptional Strength – Designed to withstand heavyweight loads with superior durability.
  • Even Load Distribution – Reduces stress points on the bag bottom, ensuring enhanced longevity.
  • Square & Stable Structure – Maintains a square shape due to vertical side seams, improving stacking efficiency.
  • Ideal for Fine Materials – Perfect for storing fine-grain products that require minimal sifting.
  • Strict Quality Control – Manufactured with precision and high-quality standards for reliability.
  • Versatile Usage – Commonly used for storing minerals, oxide powder, titanium, and plastic resins.
  • Multi-Industry Application – Suitable for aluminum waste, rubber mulch, construction soil, and more.
  • Enhanced Stackability – Ensures efficient storage and transportation with excellent stability.
