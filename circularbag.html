CIRCULAR BAGS

Circular Bag

Circular or Tubular Bags are a widely used FIBC (Flexible Intermediate Bulk Container) design, ideal for packaging solid, dry bulk materials. These bags are crafted from a single continuous piece of fabric, forming a cylindrical shape, with the top and bottom sewn using flat panels. This seamless vertical structure enhances durability and prevents leakage.

Thanks to their tubular design, these bags offer increased load capacity and efficient space utilization, allowing them to be securely stacked. The absence of vertical seams not only makes them spacious and strong but also reduces the risk of product leakage.

As a versatile alternative to four-panel bags, Circular Bags are constructed using a circular loom, ensuring a smooth, confined shape with precise cut adjustments. Their linerless design makes them an excellent choice for storing fine and moisture-sensitive materials. Although named circular, these bags naturally take a square shape when filled, optimizing storage and transport efficiency.
