Mesh Bags

Mesh Bag Designs

RJ International is a well-established manufacturer of mesh bags, renowned for offering a diverse range of high-quality packaging products. Our mesh bags are extensively utilized for packaging various produce, including onions, cabbage, citrus fruits, and more.

Crafted from durable materials, these bags are designed to allow optimal air circulation, which helps prevent moisture buildup and extends the shelf life of the contents. Beyond produce storage, our mesh bags are versatile and can be used for laundering delicate items, organizing children's toys, or even as beach bags. By choosing RJ International's mesh bags, you contribute to reducing plastic waste, as they are reusable and environmentally friendly.
